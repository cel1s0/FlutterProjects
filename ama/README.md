# Ask Me Anything - AMA
A simple application that displays images randomly. It will give you the answers to all the tricky questions in life. You can make the ball change at the press of a button. 
Answers:
	- YES
	- NO
	- ASK AGAIN LATER
	- THE ANSWER IS YES
	- I HAVE NO IDEA
